Hover on the constellation of text phrases and follow them down to find GPT-2's interpretation of the universe as a concept, GAN's interpretation as images of spaces, and image identifier's translation of them.


ABOUT

An experimental "uncreative daisy chain" in Runway ML's available AI models.

A starter phrase by my own creation was fed into a GPT-2 text generator. I broke up the text into reasonably-sized clauses and fed each snippet into AttnGAN, an environmental image generator, one at a time. I then ran each generated image into a GAN image identifier, trained to identify spaces. As a final experiment, I put the entire text written by the GPT-2 generator into AttnGAN, creating the image you see below.
