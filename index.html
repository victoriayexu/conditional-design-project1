ABOUT

Beyond the observable universe

and

into the voids

which lay down,

maddening us

and

making our way through the undiminished space,

and

without doing that,

ever abiding by the same principles.

And if it is ever released

by a 'super universe',

then what must be the reason why

and

why would it be necessary to live

in this unclenchable universe

beyond this

unclenchable, unending momentum of knowledge?











The motions

of

the body of the cosmos

are

among them,

for which they are present

as

the debris

of

all eternity.

The universe

is

a benign sphere

of

direct and invisible force.

That, in the visible world,

is

the true force

of

nature.

Victoria Xu
